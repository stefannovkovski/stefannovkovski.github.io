At the age of 6 I started school at Brakja Miladinovci in Kumanovo, where I formed lasting friendships and fond memories that would stay with me for years to come.The school provided a nurturing environment that encouraged curiosity and fostered a love for learning. I vividly recall the colorful classrooms, engaging teachers, and the joyous atmosphere that greeted me each day.

Throughout my time at Brakja Miladinovci, I was exposed to a wide range of subjects, from mathematics and languages to science and arts, allowing me to explore my diverse interests and develop a well-rounded knowledge base. The dedicated teachers played a pivotal role in my educational journey, patiently guiding me through complex concepts, sparking my curiosity, and igniting a passion for knowledge.

I went to different competitions like biology, chemistry, physics, informatics, math, english ...

2009 - 2018

Going from the familiar hallways of elementary school to the uncharted territories of high school marked a significant milestone in my educational journey. As I stepped through the doors of Gimnazija Goce Delcev in Kumanovo, I embarked on a thrilling new chapter filled with opportunities for growth and self-discovery.

I was in the class that has more math - PMA. I encountered challenges that tested my resilience and perseverance. However, these hurdles served as stepping stones for personal growth and character development. The supportive environment and encouragement from both my peers and teachers propelled me to overcome obstacles, fostering a sense of determination and resilience that would prove invaluable in my future endeavors.
When I graduated I knew that I will have many memories to take with me because I have made friends for life in the time I spent at my high school.

2018 - 2022

Entering the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje marked the beginning of an exciting journey for me. As a passionate individual with a deep interest in technology, I was eager to immerse myself in the world of computer science. That's why I chose software engineering and information systems as my study field.

My first year at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje has been an enriching and transformative experience. This journey has fueled my passion for technology and has motivated me to pursue further studies and explore exciting opportunities in the field. As I look forward, I am confident that the faculty will shape me into a competent and successful professional software engineer.
